Our Mission

Community Patrol NZ has a vision of a safer New Zealand. Our contribution to that is through actively patrolling communities to prevent crime and reduce harm. In partnership with the Police, our more than 4,000 patrollers across Aotearoa New Zealand help keep Kiwis and visitors safe.

Community Patrols are organised and operated by their local communities and raise their annual operating expenses through local grants, sponsorship and donations.
