Volunteer

Our patrol members are fully trained to NZ Police and National Community Patrols New Zealand (CPNZ) protocols.

Volunteering with the HACP is a great way to help make your community safer.

We are looking for motivated and committed volunteers for at least one four-hour shift a month.

Requirements

  • ✔ 18 years or older
  • ✔ Adhere to Police protocols
  • ✔ Full Driver’s Licence
  • ✔ Fit & Healthy
  • ✔ Fluent in English
  • ✔ Must pass Police vetting
  • ✔ Digital Literacy
  • ✔ Ability to communicate effectively
  • ✔ Friendly and honest nature

What we offer

  • ✅ Online training modules
  • ✅ On-patrol training with senior patrollers
  • ✅ Pathway to Police

Previous experience is not required as on-the-job training is given.
